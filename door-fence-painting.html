Our Exterior Process

1. Prep & Wash

We power wash or hand scrub surfaces to remove dirt, mildew, and loose paint.

2. Sand & Repair

We scrape peeling areas and sand smooth. Minor wood repairs included.

3. Prime

High-bond bonding primer is applied to bare wood or metal for adhesion.

4. Finish Coat

Two coats of premium exterior paint (Sherwin-Williams/Benjamin Moore).

Tip: Paint vs. Stain for Fences?

Stain penetrates the wood, showing the grain and never peeling (it just fades).
Paint creates a solid "shell" offering thicker protection and unlimited colors, but may peel after 5-7 years.
We can help you decide what's best for your property!
