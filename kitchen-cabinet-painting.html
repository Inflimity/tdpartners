Our Factory-Finish Process

1

Removal & Labeling

We carefully remove all doors, drawers, and hardware, labeling them for perfect re-installation.

2

Off-Site Spraying

Doors are taken to our facility. We clean, degrease, sand, prime, and spray them in a controlled environment for a flawless finish.

3

On-Site Box Painting

While doors dry, we prep and paint the cabinet boxes (frames) in your home, masking off everything to protect your floors and walls.

4

Re-Installation

We return, re-hang doors, install hardware (old or new), and ensure everything closes perfectly.
